### Item 3: Sprint, Standup, Retrospect keywords
- **Introduced by:** Audria (TA)
- **Discussion:** 
  - Sprints: Lasts 1-2 weeks for this class, Lasts ~1 month in industry depending on circumstances. Its main purpose is to break down the timeline to smaller timelines to create smaller managable tasks that can be completed within a reasonable time.
  - Standups: 3x a week, quick ~15 minute meetings to discuss individual task updates, where everyone stands, and who needs help on what. Everyone should be honest about their progress and what they need help with.We can decide how the standups are scheduled. TA recommends standups IRL before or after class TuTh and the third one being scheduled around our availability virtually (Slack/Zoom) or in person. 
  - Retrospect / Reflective Activity: Takes place after each sprint on how the process was, what tools were used. Its purpose is to reflect on the sprint, not blame or complain. Use some model like start, stop, continue (what should we start/stop/continue to improve the workflow) or mad, sad, glad (what we are mad/sad/glad about in the workflow). Try to go in with suggestions to improve the group. It's recommended to have a retrospective after each sprint, but if there's a flow going on in between some sprints, it may not be necessary.
- **Resolution / Status:** Resolved — keywords and flow clarified
